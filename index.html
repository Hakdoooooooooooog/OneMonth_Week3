Ado

Ado (アド, born October 24, 2002) is a Japanese singer.

She is signed to Sony Music Japan's record label Epic Records Japan.

In 2020, at the age of 17, she made her debut with the digital single titled "Usseewa", which peaked at number 1 on Billboard Japan Hot 100, Oricon Digital Singles Chart, and the Oricon Streaming Chart.

Most Iconic Song

Usseewa

Usseewa (ウッセーわ) is the debut single by Japanese singer Ado. It was released on September 30, 2020, by Epic Records Japan. The song was written by Ado, and produced by Ado, and Kento Nakajima. The song was released in two versions: a standard version and a limited edition version.

Limited edition version includes a music video for the song. The song was released digitally on September 30, 2020, and physically on October 7, 2020. The song debuted at number one on the Billboard Japan Hot 100, Oricon Digital Singles Chart, and the Oricon Streaming Chart.
